Welcome to my Blog!

Posted on January 1, 2021 by Claire Hill
Chef Authentic Foods Around The World

My name is Claire Hill and Ive been a food blogger for the past 5 years. I used to work as a head chef for a prominent restaurant known as “The Hidden Gem” in the UK – and Now I spend my time looking for hidden gems for that good food experience, Learning more everyday…Living my best life!

I want to share all these experiences with you, so that one day, you can pop on by and live them for yourself. So for now, Live vicariously and be sure Like my page & share my Hidden Gems to family and friends. In the meantime, I am going to try my best to convert you all to be a Food Fanatix – Just like me!

So where in the world did I find myself this week? Well I’m glad you asked, I visited South Africa, Cape Town, also known as “The Mother City”. South Africa is known to be a truly diverse country, nicknamed the “Rainbow Nation” with 11 official languages. I gave a restaurant known as “Foodies Corner” a visit, they claim to bring a 5 star around the world experience with a few authentic food to serve up to.

I started with a Mutton Bunny Chow, basically it’s bread with a mutton curry with potatoes served in it. It was a blast full of flavour with such tender meat too. I highly recommend, got my stamp of approval. I must say, the weather in Cape Town is quite a chilly one, supper at this restaurant was beef stew with vegetables, it was absolutely delicious. I ended off with Baklava, they added a bit of turkey here, I took a to go bag which consisted of a meat doner, wrapped up in fresh vegetables and a tortilla wrap. Whilst touring the place, I found a small bakery, my hidden gem, known as “The Valley Bakery”, what appealed to me the most was their lamingtons, strawberry coated and chocolate coated too. Im still yet to discover more hidden gems around here, Next Week, Im heading off to a City known as “Durban” – Here I come!
